Description

Named after the renowned spring of Jiangnan, "Baisha Ancient Well," the packaging features this well as a backdrop, with a central design of auspicious double cranes symbolizing good fortune. This product continually incorporates new technologies, scientific formulations, and carefully balanced flavors to enhance its technical quality. After nearly thirty years of development, it has become a bestseller nationwide. Since 2002, "Baisha" cigarettes have ranked first in single brand production and sales for four consecutive years. Flavor profile: it offers a rich and harmonious aroma, with a pure and comfortable aftertaste.
